Instructions

The goal of the game is to win 0 rounds against your opponent.

You win a round as soon as your score is 0 points higher than your opponent's score.

You lose (and your opponent wins) a round when your snake collides with itself, the opponent's snake or one of the walls.

You gain points by eating apples that randomly appear in the game area. To eat an apple the snake needs to run over it. When you eat an apple, the snake grows and the game speed increases.

The red snake is controlled by the WASD keys, and the blue snake is controlled by the arrow keys on the keyboard.
